Mobile for campus

Empower students' smartphone with as many campus services as possible is my job and passion for the past three years.

Are you here?

Take your phone out, fire up your social account, beep! Your attendance is marked. Linking social account with campus ID, the Dean Office is able to figure out whether if you go to school or not, with magic (WiFi)

Goooo Sports!

As the core of school spirits, it's always fun to track the leadership board, watch videos and receive live comments straight from the Stadium.

Stories affirm who we are

Working with organizations from 10 high schools, anonymous users upload stories affirming who they are: lovers, friends, athletes, feminists and so much more

What about future campus?

I imagine working at the Student Government, the Student Affairs Office and the Admission Office at Bard College.
